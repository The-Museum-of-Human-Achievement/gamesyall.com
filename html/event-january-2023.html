January Meetup

January 21st, 2023 - 2-5pm

Batch Craft Beer 3220 Manor Rd, Austin, TX 78723

Free admission, donations appreciated! RSVP! Space is limited.


Happy New Year! We're kicking 2023 off with a Family Edition of Games Y'all. This daytime meetup is for everybody, but especially families!

We're showcasing a diverse roundup of games that feature makers of different ages and experience levels. We hope to connect younger folks interested in indie video games and game development with developers who are excited about their craft! Game developers in attendance will be there to share their work, chat with folks, and tell us more about what goes on behind the scenes.

Batch Craft Beer offers food and beverages on site as well as ample outdoor space for kiddos to play.


Venue + COVID Details

This event is indoors and outdoors. There is a decent sized parking lot and bike racks. We strongly encourage that everyone be vaccinated or have recently tested negative for COVID. Do not attend if you are experiencing symptoms. We’ll be in shared space, so can’t guarantee the vax status of regular venue patrons. Hand sanitizer and wipes for controllers will be available.


Games Y'all + Fantastic Arcade

Games Y’all is a meetup presented by Fantastic Arcade & The Museum of Human Achievement, held every month at venues around Austin for indie devs, digital artists, and games fans.

Fantastic Arcade celebrates, amplifies, and supports the unique and underrepresented within indie games and beyond.
