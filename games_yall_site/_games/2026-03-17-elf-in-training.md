In Elf In Training, a chaotic 4-player couch co-op game, you play as Santa’s newest recruits delivering presents. The catch? You have to break into people’s homes to do it.

Homeowners, pets, and strange Christmas entities will defend their territory, so you’ll need to sneak, dodge, and outsmart them to complete your deliveries and prove you’re ready to become one of Santa’s elfs.
