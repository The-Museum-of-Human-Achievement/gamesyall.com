This is a combat-only demo of the upcoming open world space adventure, Free Stars: Children of Infinity, a crowdfunded sequel to the 30 year old cult classic The Ur-Quan Masters (once known as Star Control II). Players each control one of a fleet of dozens of unique ships with different weapons and abilities. The final game also includes a deep and nuanced story, conversations with dozens of different aliens, exploration, customization, and resource-gathering.
