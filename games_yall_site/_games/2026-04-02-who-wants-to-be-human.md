Who Wants to Be Human? is a rogue-like match-3 where you build powerful relic combinations that turn simple swaps into massive chain reactions. Stack rule-breaking effects, create runaway synergies, and engineer explosive cascades to hit ever-increasing score targets.

Set in a twisted game show run by an unhinged demon, you play as townsfolk transformed into mice, competing to win back your humanity. The deeper you go, the more chaotic—and powerful—your runs become.

Design the system. Break the game. Let it spiral.
