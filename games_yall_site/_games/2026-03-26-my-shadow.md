Mikah is a young boy whose once warm and loving family is torn apart by constant arguments, plunging him into quiet despair. Players step into his shoes to explore a distorted, dreamlike world shaped by his emotions. By searching for meaningful objects, fragments of happier memories, they can push back the darkness and uncover the truth behind the nightmare. Each discovery helps Mikah confront his inner fears and find a path toward healing.
