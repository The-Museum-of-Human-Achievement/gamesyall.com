In Detective Academy, observation is everything! As a rookie detective in training, you’ll take on a series of levels designed to test your focus, memory, and attention to detail. Each scene is filled with items to spot and recall under pressure. The academy’s Chief and senior detectives will track your progress as you rise through the ranks. Stay sharp, prove your skills, and show you’ve got what it takes to become a top investigator!
