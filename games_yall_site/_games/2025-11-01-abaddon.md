Abaddon is a dungeon crawler inspired by old 80's adventure games like Moria and Legends of Zelda. As you explore the destroyed world you learn more about its past and people as a failure of the gods. Dive into the caves, fight creatures of the depth, and complete quests to help save the world from its own darkness.
