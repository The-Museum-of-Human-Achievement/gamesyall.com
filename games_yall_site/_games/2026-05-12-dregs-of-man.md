Dregs of Man is a roguelite survival FPS where you play as a lone soldier who's taken refuge in an abandoned trenchline, endlessly attempting to survive waves of a mindless horde. As they explore no-mans land for traps and tools to try and bolster their defense they must also deal with stragglers from the horde.
