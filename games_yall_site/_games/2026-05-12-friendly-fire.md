Friendly Fire is a casual FPS with elements of aim training and visual novel storytelling, designed to guide players who want to get into competitive FPS games. As players complete levels, talk to our wacky characters, and gamble their coins away, they are also taught how to use abilities commonly found in competitive shooters. The characters provide funny dialogue while revealing more about the world and why they are there to train the player.
