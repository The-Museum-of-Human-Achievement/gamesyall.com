In this short cyberpunk mystery, step into the shoes of Sam Burnout as he investigates his grandmother’s violent disappearance. Navigate a filth-drenched world to gather clues and interrogate neighbors to uncover the truth. Developed by a dedicated duo, the game features a striking, unique art style and a hand-crafted narrative. Experience an intimate, atmospheric story where every conversation brings you closer to unraveling the mystery.
