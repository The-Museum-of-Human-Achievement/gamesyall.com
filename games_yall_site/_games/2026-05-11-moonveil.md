Moonveil is a cozy fantasy adventure game where players use elemental magic to restore life to magical islands. Grow magical crops, fish, mine rare gems, craft useful items, and battle corrupted creatures as you work to heal ancient world trees scattered across the islands. Players explore interconnected islands, uncover new resources, and strengthen their spells while bringing balance back to the world. Moonveil blends relaxing farming gameplay with light exploration, combat, and magical world restoration in a vibrant fantasy setting.
