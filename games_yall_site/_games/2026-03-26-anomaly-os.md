ANAMOLY.OS is a narrative-driven investigation game where every decision carries weight. You play as a freelance Anomalous Entity Investigator, responding to emails from individuals experiencing strange and often dangerous phenomena—from cryptids to reality distortions. Using a digital anomaly guide, you must correctly identify each case and choose how to respond.
