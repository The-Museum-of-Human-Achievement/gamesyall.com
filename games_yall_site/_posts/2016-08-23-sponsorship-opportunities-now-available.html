Looking for a tax-deductible way to get your name out to Austin's community of independent games makers and fans? Opportunities are now available!

Following JUEGOS RANCHEROS' transition into a 501(c)(3) non-profit, we have just made sponsorship packages available for both our regular monthly events, as well as our yearly flagship festival, Fantastic Arcade.

To learn more about our sponsorship levels, you can visit our new sponsorship section, or click here for a PDF version of the same.

Just want to make a tax-deductible donation at a lower level? We can now easily accommodate that as well: click here to donate directly to JUEGOS RANCHEROS via PayPal, or use the new donate button on the sidebar!

Your donations help JUEGOS RANCHEROS grow our events and our audience in Austin, help us construct our line of arcade cabinets with brand new games, and bring amazing artists and developers from around the world right here to Austin.

If you have any questions about any of our opportunities, don't hesitate to get in touch by email at juegosrancheros@gmail.com and we'll be in touch to talk more!
