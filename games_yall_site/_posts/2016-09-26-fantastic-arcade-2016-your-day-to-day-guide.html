The time has come for the seventh-annual Fantastic Arcade, and this year we're doing things a little bit differently! In order to gauge how big our audience will be, you'll need to get a free ticket for all of our Fantastic Arcade events.

To make this process easier, we've split all of our events into individual blocks, which are listed in full below, split out day by day. If you'd like to see one of these events, simply visit the South Lamar Alamo Drafthouse ticket counter, and ask for a ticket to that specific block!

You do not need to have a Fantastic Arcade badge to get one of these tickets -- all of our events are all ages and open to the public!

Can't make it to Fantastic Arcade? Get all of the original games created for this year's festival (like nium, above) with our exclusive Fantastic Arcade 2016 bundle, with new games from the creators of DOWNWELL, KATAMARI DAMACY and much more!

Below the fold is our day-by-day guide to all of our events -- pick out any & all blocks you want to see and pick up your tickets any day this week!

[Thanks to this year's Fantastic Arcade sponsors: Sony PlayStation, The City of Austin Cultural Arts Division, JUEGOS RANCHEROS, RAZER, Amazon Lumberyard and Devolver!]

Monday, September 26th

FANTASTIC ARCADE BLOCK ONE
SEP 26, 11:00AM, ALAMO SOUTH LAMAR, THEATER 3

The first block of talks for Fantastic Arcade 2016 opens with Tijmen Tio and Tom van den Boogaart of Dutch game collective Sokpop, showing off their original creation for Fantastic Arcade, Lassos, They'll be joined by Adriaan de Jongh & Sylvain Tegroeg, presenting the whimsically illustrated Hidden Folks.

To ensure a seat at this free event, ask for a ticket for Arcade Block One at the ticket counter, no badge is required. 

FANTASTIC ARCADE BLOCK TWO
SEP 26, 2:00PM, ALAMO SOUTH LAMAR, THEATER 3

Come check out Arcade showcase games Burrito Galaxy 65 with SwackSoft softWorks workShop and West of Loathing with co-creator Zack Johnson.

To ensure a seat at this FREE event, ask for a ticket for Arcade Block Two at the ticket counter, no badge is required.

FANTASTIC ARCADE BLOCK THREE
SEP 26, 5:00PM, ALAMO SOUTH LAMAR, THEATER 3

Our first tournament of Fantastic Arcade 2016 will set the stage for this year's Starcade. Multibowl, by Alec Thompson and Bennett Foddy, is a new experimental digital blender that takes hundreds games both classic and obscure and weaves them into a collage of timed two player challenges, in Starcade style!

We'll select our Starcade challengers this year with a prelim tournament, and two will go on to compete on the final day of Arcade in the Highball!

To ensure a seat at this free event, ask for a ticket for Arcade Block Three at the ticket counter, no badge is required.

FANTASTIC ARCADE OPENING PARTY
SEP 26, 9:00PM
Empire Control Room, 606 E. 7TH STREET AUSTIN, TX 78701

Come celebrate the opening day of Fantastic Arcade with live DJ sets and games, including an exclusive peek into three PlayStation VR games: Enhance Games' Rez Infinite (!), Drool's Thumper, and Kokoromi’s PlayStation VR game SUPERHYPERCUBE!

Sponsored with help from our friends at Sony PlayStation.

Wednesday, September 28th

FANTASTIC ARCADE BLOCK NINE
SEP 28, 11:00AM, ALAMO SOUTH LAMAR, THEATER 3

Join us for our second annual panel of hardware amusements- games and distractions that toss the screen and invade the arcade. This year we're excited to have DIY tinkerers Mickey Delp and Alan Watts let us peek into their laboratories and explain this year's mad arcade creations, plus Randy Smith, who has created the custom adventure game Knight Brite for Mickey's new hardware while it's still being created.

Following close behind, Andy "Batsly Adams" Reitano, creator of last year's audience choice award winning Super Russian Roulette will be returning with he and Zachary "Zachstronaut" Johnson's massively-glitchable NESpectre. Don't miss this block!

To ensure a seat at this free event, ask for a ticket for Arcade Block Nine at the ticket counter, no badge is required.

Thursday, September 29th

FANTASTIC ARCADE BLOCK THIRTEEN: FANTASTIC MOTION VIDEO
SEP 29, 12:00AM, ALAMO SOUTH LAMAR, THEATER 3

FANTASTIC FEST FULL MOTION VIDEO!

Join Sagan Yee (Toronto’s Hand Eye Society) and Peter Kuplowsky (Fantastic Fest) on an outrageous adventure through the short-lived, scan line-ridden era of Full Motion Video games and “interactive cinema”!

We’ve resurrected several movie/game chimeras created at the height of Hollywood’s FMV hubris for this historical presentation (featuring several big stars in unfortunate roles), as well as spliced together our own grotesque experiment set on the grounds of Fantastic Fest itself. Experience a collective playthrough of FANTASTIC FEST: THE GAME on the big screen, the way it was meant to be seen!

To ensure a seat at this free event, ask for a ticket for Arcade Block 13 at the ticket counter, no badge is required.
