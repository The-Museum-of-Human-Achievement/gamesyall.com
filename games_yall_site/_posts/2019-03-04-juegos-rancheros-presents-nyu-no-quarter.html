JUEGOS RANCHEROS is excited to share NYU Game Center's 2018 games from No Quarter. Join us WEDNESDAY, March 6th at 7:00 PM at Austin's North Door for a selection of commissioned games by NYU.

[RSVP FOR THIS EVENT AND INVITE YOUR FRIENDS ON FACEBOOK HERE]

The selected artists include - Brianna Lei, Ivan Safrin, Ethan Redd, and Ayla Myers.

You can read more about NYU's No Quarter Exhibition here:
https://gamecenter.nyu.edu/events/no-quarter/no-quarter-2018/

In addition to our favorites, we’ll also be showcasing more multi- and single-player games by the game’s developers, on top of the usual ‘indie alley’ games brought in by Austin’s local game community!

You can play these games and more Wednesday, March 6th, at 7:00PM at North Door, 501 Brushy Street, Austin, TX 78702, with a short introduction at 8:00PM. The show is free, all ages, and open to all the public, so come join us as we drink, play and meet the people changing the way we think about games!
